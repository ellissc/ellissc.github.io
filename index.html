Ellis Cain

I am senior at Indiana University studying Cognitive Science (B.S.) and East Asian Languages and Culture (Chinese, B.A.). I work in Dr. Chen Yu's Developmental Intelligence Lab on the Human Simulation Paradigm project using computational methods to study language learning, which is used to explore and model statistical learning in infants. I am interested in exploring topics such as language acquisition, the intersection between language and cognition, computational models of cognition and meaning representation, as well as computational linguistics and natural language processing, including semantic analysis and word representation, authorship verification, and computer vision methods.
